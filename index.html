About

MO

A designer who likes to get her hands in the code.

I started out as a print designer, fell hard for the web, and never looked back. These days I split my time between shaping product strategy and prototyping in the browser, because the best way to know if an idea works is to feel it move.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me restoring an old film camera, over-watering my plants, or losing an argument with my cat about who owns the desk chair.
